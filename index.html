ABOUT PROJECT K

Project K: Kasandigan Hub – The Calambeños’ Guide is a meaningful initiative led by the youth of Calamba, designed to serve as a guide for citizens—especially the younger generation—on opportunities, events, and vital information about the city of Calamba.

Initially, its purpose was to assist older individuals in better navigating technology and digital services. However, as time passed, the project expanded and evolved with various innovative ideas to better address the needs of the community.

Among its key features are updates on job opportunities, student guides to schools and educational programs, and informative content on the history and notable destinations within Calamba. One of the project’s main highlights is the ID Generator, which allows members of Kasandigan ng Kabataan to obtain their official digital ID in a faster and more organized manner.

Kasandigan ng Kabataan is a youth-led, non-governmental organization composed of young leaders from different barangays of Calamba. All their initiatives are volunteer-driven and without compensation, with a strong focus on raising awareness, compassion for others and the environment, and the promotion of peace and progress throughout the city.

Ultimately, Project K: Kasandigan Hub is a significant step toward a more informed, more organized, and more modern Calamba—led by the youth, for the youth, and for the entire Calambeño community.

Disclaimer: This project is not intended to steal identity or organizational data, but rather to provide valuable information to the youth of Calamba—knowledge that can help them gradually grow and develop in their respective paths.
